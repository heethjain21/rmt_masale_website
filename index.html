RMT Industries Pvt Ltd is one of the leading manufacturer, supplier and exporter of a wide range of spices in India. Established in the year 2012, RMT Masale have specialised themselves in various types of spices giving a perfect blend to the Indian Ethnic Food.

The advantageous location of Central India has enabled it to have a wide distribution channel and an advanced Supply Chain Management System.

The high quality of spices and its affordable price makes our spices the most preferred company throughout India and increasing export demands across the globe.
